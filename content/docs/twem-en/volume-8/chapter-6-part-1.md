# Chapter 6 – [Time Heaven] Eleonora I

While Fran was fighting Alogis, the First Demon King, Finne and the others were fighting against a horde of monsters.

“Finne, we’re heading there too!”

Several monsters had evaded Iris’ attacks and were approaching Finne.

However, Finne didn’t panic and simply killed the monsters that attacked her.

“Iris, we’re good here!”

Iris nodded and focused on her battle.

Activating her unique skill, “Gale Rush,” which transformed her into an incarnation of lightning, she moved around at high speed and defeated monsters one after another.

Iris and Finne were not the only ones who were active on this battlefield.

Asha, Kuzel, and Charlotte also fought well against the approaching hordes of monsters and the fallen angels under Lucifer.

“Are you okay?”

Kuzel rushed to the soldier who was about to succumb and decapitated the monsters.

The soldier was mesmerized for a moment by Kuzel’s figure with her bright red hair flowing in the wind.

“Quickly retreat and get yourself patched up.”

“But, facing this many alone…”

“If it’s just this much, I’ll be fine. Besides, there’s Charlotte with me.”

Kuzel turned around, and the soldier followed her gaze, and there he saw Charlotte, who had elegantly taken down a monster with a single blow.

Charlotte noticed Kuzel and the others and came towards them.

“Kuzel, please don’t leave me behind.”

“Maybe you’re just slow, Charlotte?”

“Who was the one who left their post saying, ‘There are a lot of monsters over there!’ and left?”

“Ugh, well, that’s…”

Kuzel averted her gaze from Charlotte.

“Well, we’ve defeated most of the monsters in our area, so that’s fine…”

Charlotte looked as if she wanted to say, “Really?”, then cleared her throat, regained her composure, and issued an order to the soldiers around her.

“I am Charlotte von Galzio, the princess of the Galzio Empire. Everyone around, please retreat for a moment. We’ll take care of this.”

“But there are too many of them!”

“There’s no need to worry.”

Facing the approaching horde, Charlotte unsheathed her golden sword, the Yamabuki Sword, and raised it to the sky.

“—Bloom in full glory, Yamabuki!”

As Charlotte spoke, golden Yamabuki flowers blossomed in the air.

And then, Charlotte swung her sword down, and Yamabuki petals were shot one after another at the approaching horde of monsters.

Then, Kuzel also poured magic power into the Camellia Flower Sword that Haruto had given her, and slashed at the horde of monsters one after another.

The horde of monsters were cut to pieces and blown away by the flying slashes, leaving them helpless.

The soldiers were speechless at the sight.

Charlotte informed the soldiers.

“As you can see, we’re fine on our own. So, injured people please wait in the rear, and those who can move, head to reinforce the others.”

“Understood! Good luck!”

The soldiers saluted and ran off.

“Well then, Kuzel.”

“Yeah, Char.”

The corners of their mouths turn up slightly.

“–Let’s fight to our heart’s content!”

The two of them ran towards the horde.

◇◇◇

Meanwhile, in the back, Suzuno, Ephyr, and Ilmina were treating the injured.

Many of the injured were missing limbs.

However, the three were making the most of the equipment Haruto had given them, and were doing a great job.

“My arm!”

A man missing his right arm was brought in front of Suzuno.

The wound hadn’t healed, and he was bleeding profusely and on the verge of death.

Suzuno rushed over and activated a healing spell.

Normally, healing spells wouldn’t regenerate missing parts, but she was using a higher level of holy magic. Moreover, thanks to the ability of the rapier that Haruto had made for her, White Beauty Alterna, which tripled the effects of holy magic, it was even possible to regenerate missing parts.

“It’s a miracle from God…! An angel!”

“I’m just good at healing magic. Ahahaha…”

Suzuno smiled awkwardly at the grateful soldier.

A little distance away, Ilmina was treating the seriously injured.

“I-I can’t do this anymore. I’ll stay here…”

“Don’t say that. I’ll heal you!”

The staff she held in her hand – the Holy Heaven Eumenides, also made by Haruto – was pointed at the seriously injured man’s chest.

“—Heal.”

![](https://web.archive.org/web/20250522161619im_/https://i0.wp.com/shintranslations.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Ilmina.jpeg?w=1512&ssl=1)

As Ilmina chanted, the terrible wounds quickly healed, and even the missing parts were regenerated.

The man shed tears at this miraculous sight.

“It’s a miracle. You are a saintess after all…!”

“Although I’m called a saintess… I’m glad I was able to help you.”

“Oh! Now I can fight again!”

The man smiled and left the tent with his weapon in hand.

A little distance away, Ephyr was treating the injured.

She could also use healing magic, though not to the same extent as Suzuno and Ilmina, and as an elf, she had a high amount of magic power and the ability to manipulate it.

Using these strengths, she deployed magic over a wide area and healed the injured in one go.

“Amazing. All these people at once… um, is your magic power okay?”

“Don’t worry. I can still go on. Next place, please!”

“Okay. But don’t push yourself. Next is this way!”

In this way, Suzuno, Ephyr, and Ilmina were doing great work in the rear.
